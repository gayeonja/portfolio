Find My Classmates is an Android app for students to connect with classmates, access professor ratings, register for classes, and view feedback on courses, streamlining class-related information. The project had three major parts: user, courses, and chat. I was in charge of designing and making the prototype of the app and creating the user portion.

My Responsibilities

1. Create a Google Firebase database and inform teammates how to access it.
2. Code sign up, log in, and profile features and connect it to the database.
3. Design and prototype the whole app.
