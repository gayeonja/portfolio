Community is a front-end cross platform application coded using Flutter. It currently does not have a back-end but I am planning to integrate Firebase to this project soon. Try out the application by pulling the project into Android Studio and running it in a Pixel 5 emulator or Google Chrome.

How community started

FaceBook Groups is a convenient tool for many clubs on campus. I am a part of a club at USC that has utilized FaceBook Groups to provide information about upcoming events, release sign-ups, post photos that we want to share with just the members of the club, and many more. However, because FaceBook is simply not popular to our generation, many students have refused to make a FaceBook to join our group or make an account and join the group but completely forget about it. Currently we use Instagram to promote our events and linktree to post sign-up links, but because our Instagram account is public, we still use FaceBook Groups to post things that we want private to club members. And because most of our club members do not use FaceBook daily, they miss out on these posts. My idea for this app came from the thought that it would be nice if we can move what we do on Instagram and FaceBook to one simple app made for just groups like our club.
